Jupiter

Did you know, Jupiter was named after the Roman Ruler of the Gods?

Jupiters Big Red Spot is a storm that reaches down 300 miles! Now thats deep.

One day on Jupiter goes by in just 10 hours. Talk about a hectic day. You'd get nothing done!

Did you know that Jupiter has its own heat source? it emits more energy than it receives from the Sun. Talk about a personal heater!

Try this on for size: Jupiter is 11 times wider than Earth. If Earth were the size of a nickel, Jupiter would be the size of a basketball!

The top cloud layer of Jupiter is ammonia ice, the middle layer is likely made of ammonium hydrosulfide crystals. The inner could be water ice.
